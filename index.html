About

Turning data into decisions

I'm an aspiring Business Intelligence and Data Analyst with hands-on experience across the entire analytics pipeline — designing star-schema data warehouses, writing SQL to answer business questions, and building dashboards in Tableau, SAS Visual Analytics, and SAP Business Warehouse. My work focuses on making data usable: clean models, reproducible queries, and visuals that lead to a clear recommendation. This portfolio grew out of graduate BI coursework (MISY 5360, Lamar University) and is extended with reproducible SQL and the interactive dashboards below.
